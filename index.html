Biographie

Originally from Russia, Vahitosha began her DJ journey in Istanbul, where her distinctive sound and ability to connect with audiences quickly opened doors to new opportunities.

Following her performances in Turkey, she was invited to play at a renowned French dining destination in Dubai that seamlessly evolved into a vibrant nightlife experience after dinner, a defining moment that launched her career in the UAE.

Since then, Vahitosha has performed across Dubai's dynamic nightlife scene, holding residencies and appearing as a guest DJ at a variety of venues and events. Her growing presence behind the decks has earned her recognition for delivering sets that balance musical depth, energy and atmosphere.

Vahitosha crafts immersive house music journeys built on deep grooves, melodic elements and magnetic energy. Moving effortlessly between deep, driving and groove-driven sounds, she creates sets designed for connection, movement and unforgettable dancefloor moments.

Venues

Over the past few years, Vahitosha has performed at some of Dubai's most sought-after venues, including ICONIC DXB Boat Party (Techno & Chill), Playa Pacha, ZETA Seventy Seven, Kaspia, Iris, SushiSamba Dubai, Kinugawa Dubai, and many others.

Known for creating captivating musical journeys and unforgettable dancefloor experiences, she continues to strengthen her presence across the international electronic music scene.
